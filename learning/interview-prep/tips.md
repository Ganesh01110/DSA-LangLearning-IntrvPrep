# 📝 General Interview Tips

- **Be Concise**: Keep your answers direct and to the point.
- **Use the STAR Method**: For behavioral questions (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
- **Show Passion**: Talk about *why* you chose a certain technology or architectural pattern.
- **Ask Questions**: Always have 2-3 thoughtful questions ready for the interviewer (e.g., about their tech stack, team culture, or upcoming challenges).
